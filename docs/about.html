What is MatchProphet?

MatchProphet is a purely entertainment experiment for the 2026 FIFA World Cup. We built an AI sports pundit with zero filter, a questionable attitude, and a surprising amount of football "knowledge."

Pick any World Cup match, enter your prediction, and let our AI tell you why you're probably wrong. Then generate a shareable prophecy card to prove it to your friends.

⚠️ Important Disclaimers

  • • MatchProphet is NOT affiliated with FIFA, any national football association, or any official World Cup organization.
  • • All AI-generated predictions are for entertainment only and do not constitute betting advice, gambling tips, or financial guidance of any kind.
  • • We do not collect personal data. Predictions are generated in real-time and never stored on any server.
  • • Match data (teams, flags, schedules) is based on publicly available information. Team flags are represented by Unicode emoji flags.

🔧 Tech Stack

  • Frontend: Vanilla HTML + Tailwind CSS (CDN)
  • AI Engine: Google Gemini 3.5 Flash API
  • Share Cards: HTML5 Canvas (client-side generation)
  • Hosting: GitHub Pages
  • Data: Static match schedule (public World Cup 2026 fixtures)
